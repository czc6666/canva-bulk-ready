    One question, one small test

    Data problem or Canva-side problem?

    01

    Check

    Catch blank or duplicate headers, uneven rows, empty columns, duplicate rows, and malformed quoted fields.

    02

    Shrink

    Export the same headers with only the first three data rows. This reduces the next test without exposing your content to us.

    03

    Isolate

    If the three-row batch fails too, inspect Canva mapping, design state, batch limits, or platform health instead of endlessly cleaning the spreadsheet.
